Brand: Marra’s

Brand Evaluation

The brand Marra’s presents a simple and eye-catching design that is easy to remember. The crown in the logo suggests quality and gives the impression that the brand offers something special or premium. The green color makes the design feel fresh and lively, which can attract customers. The “Since 2020” also helps show that the business has already been around for a few years, adding a sense of trust. However, the logo could be improved by making the glow effect less strong and choosing a clearer font so it is easier to read. Adding a short tagline could also help people understand what the brand is about. Overall, Marra’s has a good and appealing concept, but with a few small improvements, it can look more professional.
